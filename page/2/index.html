change the WSL starting diectory in windows terminal
发表于2021-09-27|Windows
Open Settings, Profiles, Ubuntu-20.04(This is your WSL terminal in Windows Terminal), General in turn. Then you can see the setting of Starting directory， change the value to \\wsl.localhost\Ubuntu-20.04\home\ubuntu. This value may be different, you need to change the content of the ubuntu-20.04 section according to your WSL version. Restart your Windows Terminal and you will be see that it has taken effect.
